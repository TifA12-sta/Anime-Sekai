About

Kusuriya no Hitorigoto (The Apothecary Diaries) is a historical mystery anime adapted from the light novel series written by Natsu Hyuuga and illustrated by Touko Shino. Set in an imperial court, the story follows Maomao, a clever and curious girl who was kidnapped and forced into servitude within the inner palace.

Despite being relegated to the life of a lowly maid, Maomao's knowledge of medicine and her sharp intellect catch the attention of the palace's higher-ups. Her talent for solving complex mysteries soon leads her into the tangled web of palace intrigue, and she becomes a vital asset in uncovering hidden truths and resolving dangerous situations.

Combining elements of historical drama, intrigue, and a unique protagonist, Kusuriya no Hitorigoto has captivated viewers with its detailed world-building, complex characters, and thrilling narrative.
