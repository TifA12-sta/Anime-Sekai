About

Kaguya-sama: Love is War is a romantic comedy anime series based on the manga written and illustrated by Aka Akasaka. The story is centered around the student council of Shuchiin Academy, where two of the brightest students, Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, engage in a psychological war of love. Both are deeply in love with each other but are too proud to confess their feelings, believing that whoever confesses first will lose and become the subordinate in the relationship. As a result, they go to absurd lengths trying to make the other confess first.

The anime adaptation, produced by A-1 Pictures, aired its first season in 2019, capturing the hearts of fans with its witty humor, clever tactics, and engaging characters. The series not only offers laughs but also delves into deeper emotional moments, making it a blend of comedy and romance that resonates with a wide audience.

Featuring memorable characters like the eccentric Chika Fujiwara and the brooding Yu Ishigami, Kaguya-sama: Love is War stands out as a unique take on the romantic genre, keeping viewers on their toes with its unpredictable twists and hilarious schemes.
