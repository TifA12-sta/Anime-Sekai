About

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End tells the story of an elven mage, Frieren, who embarks on a reflective journey after the death of her companions from a decade-long adventure. Now, after the journey, she seeks to explore the meaning of time, relationships, and life itself, as she outlives her former comrades.
